01 — Self-Introduction

Who I Am

I am a final-year Mechatronics & Robotics Engineering student on a dual degree between Asia Pacific University and De Montfort University (UK). My engineering identity is built on three pillars: mechanical precision, software intelligence, and practical delivery — developed through academic projects, industry internships, and professional society leadership.

Originating from Lusaka, Zambia, I completed A-levels in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry before relocating to Kuala Lumpur. This international background has sharpened my adaptability and collaborative instincts across diverse engineering environments.

My final-year project — a markerless VR-based teleoperation system for humanoid robot arms — integrates Inverse Kinematics, Unity Engine, ROS2, and digital twin simulation into a real-time wireless control pipeline. It reflects my ambition to work at the frontier of human-robot interaction.
